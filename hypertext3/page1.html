Hey Barbie, Survive Zombie Barbieland

Barbie, you have just woken up and are hearing strange noises.

You are currently in the Dreamhouse and look outside. Turns out Barbieland has become Zombieland, infested by zombie Kens...

You must figure out how to cure the Kens and save BarbieLand.

You have decided to investigate the source of this strange noise. You follow it to your HUGE closet and see ZOMBIE KEN about to rip up your favorite pink dress.

You have two options:

  1. Defend yourself, Grab the nearest stiletto and attack the zombie Ken and then make a run for it

  2. Risk getting bit and save the dress
